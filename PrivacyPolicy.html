Introduction

Welcome to ShowScout! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage.

Information We Collect

ShowScout collects minimal information to provide you with the best experience:

  • Device Information: Basic device type and OS version for app optimization
  • App Usage Data: Anonymous analytics to improve app performance
  • AI Prompts: When using AI recommendations, your mood/preference prompts are processed but not stored
  • Local Preferences: Theme settings and favorites stored locally on your device

We DO NOT collect: Personal identification, email addresses, location data, contacts, or any sensitive personal information.

AI-Powered Features

ShowScout uses AI technology to provide personalized movie recommendations:

  • Anonymous Processing: All AI requests are anonymous with no personal identifiers attached
